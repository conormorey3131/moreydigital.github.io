How much does social media management cost in Ireland?

Which platforms do you manage?

Instagram, Facebook, TikTok and LinkedIn. We'll recommend the platforms that make the most sense for your business rather than spreading you too thin.

No long lock-ins. We work month to month, so you stay because the results are there — not because you're stuck in a contract.

Yes — we're based in Clare but manage social media for businesses right across Ireland. Everything is handled remotely with regular check-ins and reports.
