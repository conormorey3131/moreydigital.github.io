Good to know

Frequently asked questions.

Do I have to sign a contract?

There's no long-term commitment required for any of our services. We offer flexible terms and ongoing support options to suit your business. We'll discuss all the details during your free consultation.

Can I upgrade later?

Absolutely! You can start with a simple site and scale up as your business grows. We'll migrate all your content and ensure a smooth transition. Many businesses start small and expand their web presence over time.

How long does a site take to build?

Simple websites typically launch within 48-72 hours. Larger multi-page sites take 5-7 business days for a full custom build. E-commerce stores usually take 7-10 business days depending on product catalog size. We pride ourselves on fast turnaround times.

Do you work with existing websites?

Yes! We can take over maintenance of your existing website or rebuild it entirely depending on your needs. We'll assess your current site and recommend the best approach. Many clients come to us for a fresh start with better performance and design.

Do you offer custom solutions?

Absolutely. Every project we take on is tailored to the client's needs. Whether you need a simple site or a complex web application, we'll scope it out and create a custom proposal for you.

What about domain names and hosting?

We offer managed hosting as part of our services, or we can help you set up your own. We can also register a new domain or work with your existing one. We'll cover all of this during your consultation.
