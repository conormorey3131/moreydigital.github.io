Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the Starter Package?

The Starter Package includes a professionally designed 5-page website, mobile responsiveness, basic SEO setup, contact forms, social media integration, and 30 days of post-launch support. Perfect for businesses that need a professional online presence quickly.

How does Website as a Service (WaaS) work?

WaaS is our monthly subscription model with no upfront costs. You get a custom website, hosting, maintenance, regular updates, and ongoing support for one monthly fee. It's like having your own web team without the overhead. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice.

What's included in the E-commerce Subscription?

Our E-commerce Subscription includes everything in WaaS plus a full online store setup, product management, secure payment processing, inventory tracking, order management, and monthly performance reports. We handle the technical side so you can focus on selling.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my package?

Absolutely! You can upgrade from the Starter Package to a subscription model anytime. For subscription services, you can change your plan with 30 days notice. We'll help migrate all your content and ensure a smooth transition.

Do you offer custom solutions?

Yes! While our packages cover most business needs, we understand every business is unique. Contact us to discuss custom requirements, enterprise solutions, or specific functionality you need. We'll create a tailored proposal for your project.

What about domain names and hosting?

For the Starter Package, we'll help you set up hosting (additional cost). For WaaS and E-commerce subscriptions, hosting is included in your monthly fee. We can help register a new domain or work with your existing one.
