Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to sign a contract?

For the Starter Package, there's no contract - just a one-time payment plus optional monthly care. Our Website-as-a-Service and E-Commerce packages have a 6-month minimum term to ensure we can deliver ongoing value, but you can cancel anytime after that with 30 days notice.

Can I upgrade later?

Absolutely! You can start with our Starter Package and upgrade to Website-as-a-Service or E-Commerce anytime. We'll migrate all your content and ensure a smooth transition. Many businesses start small and grow into our subscription services.

How long does a site take to build?

Starter websites typically launch within 48-72 hours. Website-as-a-Service sites take 5-7 business days for a full custom build. E-Commerce stores usually take 7-10 business days depending on product catalog size. We pride ourselves on fast turnaround times.

Do you work with existing websites?

Yes! We can take over maintenance of your existing website or rebuild it entirely depending on your needs. We'll assess your current site and recommend the best approach. Many clients come to us for a fresh start with better performance and design.

Do you offer custom solutions?

Yes! While our packages cover most business needs, we understand every business is unique. Contact us to discuss custom requirements, enterprise solutions, or specific functionality you need. We'll create a tailored proposal for your project.

What about domain names and hosting?

For the Starter Package, we'll help you set up hosting (additional cost). For WaaS and E-commerce subscriptions, hosting is included in your monthly fee. We can help register a new domain or work with your existing one.
